Welcome to our hotels page! Here, you'll find a selection of some of the best hotels we have to offer.

Dar Al Tawhid Intercontinental Makkah

Located in front of Al Masjid Al-Haram, Dar Al Tawhid Intercontinental Makkah is a 5-star hotel offering luxury spacious accommodations overlooking the Grand Mosque. The hotel has spacious and elegantly-furnished guestrooms, many of which provide a panoramic view of the Holy Mosque. Each room is ornately decorated and has a range of modern amenities including flat-screen TVs with satellite channels and Wi-Fi access. Dar Al Tawhid InterContinental Makkah has 3 restaurants for guests to choose from, serving a wide range of international cuisine, as well as many Middle Eastern specialities with the magnificent view of the Holy Masjid Al-Haram.
Dar Al Tawhid is also within easy reach of Madhbaḩ Ismā‘īl and Al Jamrah al Wusţá, a 5-minute drive away. This is our guests' favourite part of Makkah, according to independent reviews.

El Minzah Hotel

The El Minzah Hotel is a 5-star establishment located in the heart of Tangier, near the old medina. It offers 2 swimming pools, a gym and panoramic views of the bay. The wellness center includes a sauna, jacuzzi and hammam. You can book massages and body treatments here. All air-conditioned, the rooms overlook the outdoor swimming pool, the sea or the patio. Bathrobes are provided.
The 2 gourmet restaurants at the El Minzah Hotel serve Moroccan and international cuisine. You can also have a drink at the bar. The El Minzah is only a few steps from the old medina and the beach.
This is our guests' favorite part of Tangier, according to independent reviews. Couples particularly appreciate the location of this property. They give it a score of 9.4 for a stay for two.

Marhaba Royal Salem Hotel

Halfway between the marina El Kantaoui and downtown Sousse, blending into a setting where the Mediterranean is in the foreground,
Hotel Marhaba Salem is a real jewel of Arabo Moorish architecture.
Its clean lines blend beautifully with vegetation that has the charm and freshness of a garden bathed by the sea and bordered by a sandy beach.
